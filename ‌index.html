About Our Cabin

Welcome to Karadi Cabin! Our cabin offers a serene and comfortable getaway, perfect for families, couples, or solo adventurers looking to enjoy the beauty of nature. Located just 9 miles and about 17 minutes from Dodge Ridge Ski Resort, you'll find peace and tranquility here.

We believe in providing a truly relaxing experience, which is why our cabin is equipped with all the modern comforts you need while maintaining a rustic charm.
